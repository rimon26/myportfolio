Who am i?

Hi! I am Al Mahmud Rimon, a builder of both worlds — the world that dazzles the eyes and the one that powers everything behind the curtain. On my journey through code, I met curly brackets, semicolons, and errors that whispered tales of logic gone wrong. They told me, "Here, every bug is a lesson, and every fix is a step forward." I started my path in the quiet land of HTML and CSS, where design speaks in color and spacing. But curiosity pulled me deeper, into the dynamic realms of JavaScript and React — where pages breathe and interfaces dance. On the other side, in the world of logic and APIs, I crafted routes in Express, guarded doors with JWTs, and stored memories in MongoDB. Even when frameworks changed and trends shifted, I stayed — building, breaking, fixing, learning. I am a Full Stack Developer, and this is my code-powered adventure.
